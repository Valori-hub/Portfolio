About me
My name is Adam and I am 22 years old. I am an aspiring programmer who has been passionately developing my skills in the world of technology since I received my first computer. At the beginning of my programming journey, I was fascinated by game development. I decided to create an adventure platformer with an interesting storyline and unique graphics, despite having almost no budget. To achieve this, I started learning C#, as the first engine I encountered was Unity. Unfortunately, over time, I ran out of time and hands to work on the project, especially in the field of model design, so the project was abandoned. My biggest dream is to secure the funds to complete this project, which I consider to be my "life project." Later, I shifted to less ambitious projects, such as mobile games. In middle school, I developed an interest in web development, and this passion continues to this day. Currently, I am focusing on learning the Angular framework and the React library. Although many of my projects lack documentation due to my neglect in maintaining my GitHub, I am working to catch up and demonstrate that my primary motivation is passion.
